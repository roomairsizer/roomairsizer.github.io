About The Battle for Better Air

The air inside your home shouldn't be a mystery, and buying an air purifier shouldn't require a degree in fluid dynamics.

Why we built this tool

For years, the air purifier industry has relied on a confusing marketing metric: "square footage." If you look at a box in a big-box store, you'll see claims like "Cleans 1,500 square feet." What the box rarely tells you is that this claim is often based on running the machine at its maximum, deafening speed, in a room with very low ceilings, achieving only one or two air changes per hour.

We believe CADR (Clean Air Delivery Rate) is the only number that matters. Our Air Purifier Sizer strips away the marketing fluff. You input your actual room dimensions and your real air quality concerns, and the calculator does the math based on verified, independent CADR ratings.

Independence and Affiliate Disclosure

The Battle for Better Air is an independent project. We do not accept sponsorships from air purifier brands, nor do we let brands pay for placement in our calculator recommendations.

We do use affiliate links. If you click a link on our site and purchase a product on Amazon, we may earn a small commission at absolutely no extra cost to you. This keeps the calculator free and our recommendations unbiased. Products are recommended because they meet the mathematical CADR threshold for your room size—not because of commission rates.

Important Limitations

While we strive for accuracy in our product database:

  • Data can change: Product availability, pricing, and specifications can change at any time. Always confirm the details directly on Amazon or the manufacturer's website before buying.
  • No medical advice: This website is for informational and educational purposes only. We provide mathematical estimates for air changes per hour. We do not provide medical advice. If you have severe allergies, asthma, or other health concerns, please consult a qualified medical professional.
