Contact & Corrections

Help us keep our data accurate and our tools independent.

The Battle for Better Air is an independent project dedicated to simplifying indoor air quality decisions. To maintain our integrity and focus, we operate with a limited contact scope.

What we cannot provide:

  • Personalized Recommendations: We cannot provide individual buying advice or "which one should I buy" consultations beyond what our calculator offers.
  • Product Support: We are not a retailer or manufacturer. For help with a specific device, warranty claims, or troubleshooting, please contact the brand directly.

How you can help

We welcome outreach for the following reasons:

  • Data Corrections: If you spot an incorrect CADR rating, a discontinued product, or a technical error in our calculator.
